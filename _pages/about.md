I am a PhD student in Mathematics enrolled in a joint doctoral program between the 
[Indian Institute of Technology Bombay](https://www.iitb.ac.in) (India) and 
[Monash University](https://www.monash.edu) (Australia). I completed my Master's degree in Pure Mathematics at 
[Ballygunge Science College](https://www.caluniv.ac.in/campuses/campus-3.html), with specialization in Abstract Harmonic Analysis and Fourier Analysis.
My PhD supervisors are [Prof. Ricardo Ruiz Baier](https://research.monash.edu/en/persons/ricardo-ruiz-baier/), [Prof. Amiya Kumar Pani](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/goa/amiya-kumar-pani), and [Prof. Harsha Hutridurga](https://hutridurga.wordpress.com/)

My research lies in the **numerical analysis of partial differential equations**, with particular interests in

- Mixed finite element methods  
- Operator preconditioning  
- Nonlinear PDEs  
- Fluid flow in porous media  

Broadly, I develop **robust numerical methods and preconditioning techniques** for problems arising in fluid flow through porous media.
